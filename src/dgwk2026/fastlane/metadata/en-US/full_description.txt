Winterkongress 2026 Schedule

Winterkongress of digital society on February 20 and 21 2026

Winterkongress is a co-production of Rote Fabrik and digital society. We thank all supporters cordially!

https://winterkongress.ch/2026/

<b>App features</b>

<ul>
	<li>View program by day and rooms (side by side)</li>
	<li>Custom grid layout for smartphones (try landscape mode) and tablets</li>
	<li>Read detailed descriptions (speaker names, start time, room name, links, ...) of sessions</li>
	<li>Search and filter through all sessions</li>
	<li>Add sessions to favourites list</li>
	<li>Export favourites list</li>
	<li>Setup alarms for individual sessions</li>
	<li>Add sessions to your personal calendar</li>
	<li>Share a website link to an session with others</li>
	<li>Keep track of program changes</li>
	<li>Automatic program updates (configurable in settings)</li>
	<li>Automatically open sessions from web links</li>
</ul>
